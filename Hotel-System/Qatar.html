DOHA TRAVEL GUIDE

Escape on a journey to Qatar, with its capital city Doha rapidly becoming one of the world's leading destinations. While holding its rich past dear, Qatar is evolving to become one of the hot locations for business and leisure travel, and is well worth a visit.

Museums and galleries, a rich culinary scene and a myriad of activities, from perusing the items available in the local souqs to visiting some of the holy sites that Doha offers, there is plenty to see and do for everyone. Corporate guests and tourists alike will find the perfect combination of convenience and comfort at OLY Hotels & Resorts.
